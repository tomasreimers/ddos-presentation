Section 2
Symptoms of and reasons for a DDOS

A DDOS can be motivated by a variety of reasons, but all of them have one goal:

to restrict the flow of information.

Be it to damage a company
harm the reputation of an organization
or prevent access to some data
Symptoms
unavailability of a website
slow performance of a site
increase in spam mail
